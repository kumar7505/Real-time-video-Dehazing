About Our Project

Our project is an AI & ML-based dehazing system designed to enhance visibility in images, videos, and real-time camera feeds affected by fog, haze, or atmospheric disturbances.

Key Features & Capabilities

AI-Powered Dehazing Algorithm

  • Uses deep learning models to remove haze from images and videos.
  • Enhances contrast and restores natural colors.

Three Modes of Operation

  • Image Dehazing: Upload an image to remove haze.
  • Video Dehazing: Process a video file to enhance visibility.
  • Real-Time Dehazing: Use a live camera feed to perform real-time dehazing.

Future Enhancements

  • Implement adaptive AI-based dehazing for different weather conditions.
  • Add night vision enhancement for low-light environments.
